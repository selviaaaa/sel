CAKE

Decorated or undecorated cakes are used as dessert or special dishes at wedding receptions or birthday parties. In Western-style wedding receptions and birthday parties, the cake cutting ceremony is one of the events. In Indonesia, tumpeng rice often replaces cake as a special food at various memorial ceremonies. The cake cutting event is also often replaced by the tumpeng cutting event.

Large cakes with a rectangular or round shape are intended to be cut into pieces when sold or served. A cake that is rolled and filled with butter cream or jam is called rolled sponge cake. Various types of pudding also use pieces of cake as a base which are placed on the bottom of the pan before being sprinkled with jelly.
